Privacy Policy for TruckNav Pro

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

TruckNav Pro ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Email address, name, and password when you create an account
  • Apple Sign In: Apple ID, email (which may be a private relay address), and name
  • Profile Data: Truck specifications (type, weight, height, length, hazmat status), CDL number, company name
  • Trip Data: Routes, destinations, waypoints you create
  • Hours of Service (HOS) Logs: Driving hours, duty status, rest periods
  • Expense Records: Fuel purchases, tolls, meals, and other business expenses
  • Receipt Photos: Images of receipts you upload for expense tracking

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Location Data: GPS coordinates, speed, and movement while using navigation features
  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: Features used, session duration, navigation history
  • Performance Data: Crash reports, error logs (anonymized)

2.3 Third-Party Data

  • Google Maps: Map data, traffic information, routing
  • Apple: Authentication data via Sign in with Apple
  • Payment Information: Managed by Apple via In-App Purchases (we never see your payment details)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide truck-specific GPS navigation and routing
  • Calculate and display Hours of Service compliance
  • Track and categorize business expenses for tax purposes
  • Calculate IRS mileage deductions
  • Sync your data across devices
  • Process and manage your subscription
  • Improve app performance and fix bugs
  • Provide customer support
  • Send service-related notifications (not marketing)
  • Comply with legal obligations (DOT, FMCSA requirements)

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Where We Store Data

  • Supabase: Encrypted cloud database for user accounts, trips, HOS logs, expenses
  • Your Device: Local cache for offline access, cached maps
  • Apple iCloud Keychain: Secure storage of authentication tokens

4.2 Security Measures

  • Industry-standard encryption (AES-256) for data at rest
  • TLS/SSL encryption for data in transit
  • Secure authentication with bcrypt password hashing
  • Apple Sign In uses Face ID/Touch ID biometric authentication
  • Regular security audits and updates

4.3 Data Retention

  • Active accounts: Data retained as long as your account is active
  • Deleted accounts: Personal data deleted within 30 days
  • HOS logs: Retained for 6 months per FMCSA regulations (or longer if required by your carrier)
  • Expense records: Retained for 7 years per IRS recommendations

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

5.1 We DO NOT Sell Your Data

We never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

5.2 Service Providers

We share data only with trusted service providers:

  • Supabase: Database hosting and authentication
  • Google Maps Platform: Map data and routing
  • RevenueCat: Subscription management
  • Apple: Payment processing, authentication

These providers are contractually obligated to protect your data and use it only for providing services to us.

5.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by:

  • Law enforcement or government agencies
  • Court orders or subpoenas
  • DOT or FMCSA audits (HOS logs only)
  • Protection of our rights or safety of others

5.4 Business Transfers

If TruckNav Pro is acquired or merged, your data may be transferred to the new owner. You will be notified of any such change.

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 Access and Update

  • View and edit your profile, truck settings, and preferences in-app
  • Export your trip history and HOS logs
  • Download your expense records

6.2 Delete Your Data

  • In-App: Settings → Account → Delete Account
  • Email: Contact support@trucknavpro.com
  • Data deletion is permanent and cannot be undone

6.3 Opt-Out Options

  • Location Services: Settings → Privacy → Location Services → TruckNav Pro
  • Notifications: Settings → Notifications → TruckNav Pro
  • Analytics: Settings → Privacy → Analytics

6.4 California Residents (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights:

  • Right to know what personal data we collect
  • Right to delete your personal data
  • Right to opt-out of data sales (we don't sell data)
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your rights

Contact us at privacy@trucknavpro.com to exercise these rights.

6.5 European Residents (GDPR)

If you are in the EU/EEA, you have additional rights:

  • Right to access, rectification, erasure
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to restrict or object to processing
  • Right to withdraw consent
  • Right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

7. Location Data

7.1 Why We Need Location

  • Turn-by-turn navigation
  • Truck-safe routing (avoid low bridges, weight restrictions)
  • Real-time traffic updates
  • Automatic HOS logging
  • Mileage tracking for tax deductions

7.2 Location Permissions

  • While Using the App: For navigation features
  • Always (Background): For HOS compliance and automatic trip logging
  • You can change this in iOS Settings at any time

7.3 Location Data Retention

  • Active trips: Stored until trip is completed
  • HOS logs: 6 months per FMCSA regulations
  • Mileage records: 7 years per IRS recommendations

8. Children's Privacy

TruckNav Pro is NOT intended for anyone under 18 years old. We do not knowingly collect data from children. Commercial truck driving requires a CDL (minimum age 18-21 depending on jurisdiction).

If we learn we have collected data from a child under 18, we will delete it immediately.

9. Third-Party Services

9.1 Google Maps

We use Google Maps Platform for mapping and routing. Google's privacy policy applies to map data: https://policies.google.com/privacy

9.2 Apple Services

  • Sign in with Apple: Apple's privacy policy applies
  • In-App Purchases: Apple manages all payment data
  • iCloud: If you enable iCloud sync (optional)

9.3 RevenueCat

Manages subscriptions. See https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically. Changes will be posted in-app and on our website. Significant changes will require your consent.
